ABOUT ME

WHO AM I ?

My name is Barış Ömer DÖNGEL. I was born in Eskişehir in May 2001. I studied at elementary and secondary schools in elementary schools in my neighborhood. I won Necmettin Erbakan University in the university exam and went to Konya. I met Google Developper Groups in Konya and joined the team. I am currently a student of the Management and Information Systems and also the website team leader in the GDG team.

WHAT AM I DOING ?

İnternet koskocaman bir dünya ve web siteleri bu dünyada ki mülkler oluyor. Siz de web dünyasında kendinize ait bir mülkünüz olmasını istiyor musunuz? HTML5 ve CSS3 teknolojilerini kullanarak müşterilere güzel ve ilgi çekici arayüzler tasarlıyorum.
